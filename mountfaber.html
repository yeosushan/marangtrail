Mount Faber

The first highlight on the Marang Trail would be Mount Faber!
Follow the paved walkways to enjoy spectacular views of the southern part of Singapore and the Southern Islands. Enjoy a chilled drink or some ice-cream at any of the food and beverage establishments at Faber Peak while taking in the breathtaking bird's eye view of Singapore.

Cable Car

Though not a part of the Marang Trail, Faber Peak also offers a cable car ride to Sentosa which you may be interested in!
